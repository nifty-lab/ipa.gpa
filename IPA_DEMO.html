Step 6 of 7 — Reminders & Escalations
Automated Reminder & Escalation Framework
Power Automate enforces a three-level escalation ladder. No finding is missed — owners, managers, and PMO leadership are looped in progressively.
⚡ Power Automate Escalation Ladder
📧
New Finding — Instant Notification
Email sent to Action Owner on save. Includes finding ID, type, focus area, description, and target date.
Trigger: Item Created
🔔
T–7 Days — Due Date Reminder
Auto-reminder to Action Owner 7 days before target date. Prompt to submit mitigation or update status.
Trigger: Scheduled · 7 days before due
⚠️
Level 1 — Overdue Alert (Day 0 & +7)
On due date passing with status still Open: email to Action Owner + CC Project Manager. Status auto-tagged "Escalated L1".
Trigger: Due date passed · Status = Open
🚨
Level 2 — PMT Escalation (Day +14 & +21)
7 days overdue: escalation email to Project Manager + CC PMO Director. Status flagged "Escalated L2". Weekly digest included.
Trigger: 7 days overdue · Status = Escalated L1
📊
Level 3 — PMO Escalation (Day +28)
14 days overdue: formal escalation to Project Director + CC General Managers (PMO, Assurance) with full finding summary.
Trigger: 14 days overdue · Status = Escalated L2
